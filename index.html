About · Now

I'm a senior ML engineer focused on retrieval and agentic chat — designing search/RAG systems that stay fast and accurate as indexes grow, and building assistants that are reliable enough to trust in production. I also work on shaping AI strategy: helping teams pick the use-cases that survive contact with real users.

Right now: scaling OpenSearch retrieval, sharpening hybrid-search + rerank pipelines, and producing How AI you doing? — a weekly debrief on real production AI use-cases and their limits.

Background: RLHF and domain LLMs — I've published cyber-risk Llama fine-tunes and a 32k security instruction dataset on Hugging Face, and written on preference-optimization fine-tuning (DPO/ORPO).
