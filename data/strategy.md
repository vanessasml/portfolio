# ↗ Strategy Signals — Talking Points for the Room

**For having a say in your company's AI strategy.** Concise, sourced signals framed as *what to raise* with leadership — not just news.

## Where the money says the puck is going

- **Enterprise agents move from demos to deployment** — Microsoft stood up a $2.5B "Frontier" org purely to *deploy* enterprise AI. Signal: the bottleneck is now integration + rollout, not model access. *Raise:* are we resourcing deployment/ops, not just pilots? [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/02/microsoft-launches-its-own-ai-deployment-company-with-2-5-billion-commitment/)
- **Agents get embedded in systems of record** — NVIDIA × ServiceNow shipped autonomous enterprise agents. *Raise:* which of our workflows are agent-ready, and who owns guardrails? [NVIDIA](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/servicenow-autonomous-ai-agents-enterprises/)
- **"Super Agents" unify siloed tools** — Levi's is connecting per-function agents into one interface. *Raise:* do we have an agent-integration layer, or N disconnected copilots? [PYMNTS](https://www.pymnts.com/news/artificial-intelligence/2026/super-agents-are-connecting-what-enterprise-software-kept-separate/)

## Infra & cost reality (your retrieval/serving lens)

- **Inference infra is where capital is flowing** — Together AI raised $800M. *Raise:* build vs. buy for our serving/retrieval stack. [Tech Startups](https://techstartups.com/2026/07/02/venture-capital-startup-funding-roundup-july-2-2026/)
- **Memory is the constraint** — HBM sold out for 2026; semiconductor TAM lifted toward $1.3T. *Raise:* capacity-plan index growth and GPU memory now, not Q4. [Intellectia](https://intellectia.ai/blog/ai-semiconductor-stocks-rally-july-2026)

## Adoption pattern worth copying

- **Winners map one messy process, add human review, prove time/error savings** — then expand. *Raise:* pick one high-friction workflow and instrument it before scaling AI across teams. [Agentic.ai](https://agentic.ai/news)

## What to advocate internally (your edge)

- **Eval harnesses before autonomy** — gate agents with regression-locked, no-LLM tests.
- **Retrieval cost controls** — hybrid search + rerank budgets; measure tokens per resolved query.
- **Guardrails on agent PRs** — treat autonomous code changes as a review/attack surface.
